INTEROCEAN's Key Technology in Hyperbaric Oxygen Chamber

  • 6 ATA

    Pressure-based products are effective for chronic fatigue, wound care, and relatively mild case and are commonly used for general treatments. On the other hand, 6 ATA products are superior for treating severe conditions, such as diver's disease, gas poisoning, and severe burns, and can effectively treat more acute conditions by supplying oxygen to tissues at higher pressures.

  • Mixed Gas System

    With a mixed gas system, it is possible to use a gas mixed with air or another gas instead of pure oxygen. This reduces the risk of oxygen toxicity and, can also reduce the risk of decompression sickness by mixing in helium or nitrogen.

  • Automatic breathing gas changing

    A system that automatically and briefly changes your breathing gas to an oxygen-air mixed gas while you breathe pure oxygen. This is an essentiall to prevent oxygen toxicity in patients.

  • BIBS Mask

    One hose supplies 100% pure oxygen, while the other hose expels carbon dioxide outside during exhalation. In this way, the BIBS mask is an important role in preventing the realase of carbon dioxide and oxygen into the device.

  • Automatic operation system

    Automatic ↔ manual switchable. INTEROCEAN’s own automatic system, that has been deveploed and upgraded for over 10 years, raises safety by minimizing human errer.

  • Fire Prevention System

    · In case of emergency, the oxygen cutoff function, ventilation system, and sprinkler system are automatically activated.
    · Depending on the level, the decompression and venting system are automatically triggered.
    · Fire suppression is possible using custom-made fire extinguishers installed internally.

  • Infection Prevention

    · With the press of a button, the chamber interior and the respiratory exhaust system are automatically disinfected
    · Minimizes the risk of cross-infection and ensures sterilization quality through standardized disinfection procedures.

  • Entertainment

    You can watch TV, Youtube, Movie, dramas, and more during the treatment and display them connect with the treatment table.

  • Individual Heating and Cooling System

    · Provides a customized temperature environment for patients · Cools and heats only the space in use, saving energy · Improves maintenance efficiency with individual seat control

  • Recliner

    The ergonomically designed seat and backrest make you feel comfort even for long hours of sitting. It offers customized comfort for patients with adjustable reclining angles and footrest adjustments

  • EES (Easy Equalizing System)

    · Prevent Barotrauma through pulse driven pressure control method
    · Improves ear pressure adaptability in both pressurized and decompressed sections, making it applicable to many people
    · The treatment time can be shortened with the ‘Skip’ function when the patient adaptation is completed

  • Automatic Air-break

    INTEROCEAN’s own Air pressure and Air break system. It prevents the risk of oxygen poisoning and Co2 poisoning along with the internal mask (BIBS) system

  • The automated voice guidance system

    · The voice guidance system converts the instructions into voice as the operator writes and transmits them to the patient through the speakers inside the chamber
    · Automated voice guidance implemented simultaneously depends on different treatment tables
    · Install an entertainment monitor for patient convenience during treatment

  • Flexible table modifying and switching

    Able to modify and switch the table based on the medical staff’s decision without stopping the device.

  • Continuous program updates

    We completed the development of the automatic program 10 years ago, and we have continued to update and secure the program based on our operating know-how. The system has a three-stage manual safety system
